DESPERATION

Oh wait...we mean motivation. Our team was connected by our love for San Diego and food. We all enjoy using Yelp to search for food but it lacks our need to search for things to do. We decided to create a web application that would deliver what we believe to be the ultimate solution.


Problem it solves

Visualizing trips, figuring out what to do before/after/during drinking
Condenses and simplifies “things to do in san diego”


Questions it answers

Sorting SD out by general locations


Intended Audience

People new to San Diego
People looking for something to do in San Diego

Our Design Process

  • Tuesday, May 30th, 2017

    Brainstorm We began to brainstorm project ideas. What was our motivation? What kind of APIs could we use?

  • Battle of the APIs We narrowed down to two ideas and decided that we would choose based on who could make the API for each project to work first. That was the begining of a karaoke webpage.
  • Pen and Paper

  • Thursday, June , 2017

    Let the struggles begin Once we dug deeper into the Spotify API, we realized that the object that it returned did not contain the full music video but rather a 30 second preview.
  • Back to square one. We decided to table our karaoke idea and come up with a new idea.

  • Tuesday, June , 2017

    New beginings APIs are finally working in our favor. Now we've also began to work on the front end.

  • Saturday, June 10 , 2017

    Demo Day And here we are today!
